MindBench AI is an actionable platform designed to provide easily accessible and interpretable information about LLMs and LLM-based tools in mental health contexts. Developed in partnership with NAMI and building on a decade of experience from MINDapps.org, we offer transparent, real-time evaluation data to help patients, clinicians, developers, and regulators make informed decisions about AI use in mental healthcare.

Profile Evaluation
Systematic assessment of technical properties, privacy policies, data retention practices, conversation memory management, and security certifications that impact user safety and trust in mental health applications.
Performance Benchmarking
Standardized evaluation of LLM clinical reasoning skills, therapeutic response generation, and mental health task performance using domain-specific benchmarks and expert-rated assessments.
